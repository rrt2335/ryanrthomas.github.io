About

I am a passionate and resourceful software developer willing to take on new challenges and solve complex problems, working well on solo projects or on a team. I am proficient at building full-scale applications from scratch utilizing a variety of programming languages.

I have been building applications using HTML5 & CSS3, JavaScript ES6, Web API / HTTP, JS Frameworks, Vue.js, Node.js, DotNet C#, SQL, and MongoDb. Prior to software development, I spent most of my life as a musician, receiving my B.M. in Music Education from Boise State University and M.M. in Music Performance from the University of Idaho. After completing my education, I worked with Royal Caribbean International for four years. I am excited for the challenges that will come with this field and to have the opportunity to start fresh and learn valuable skills.

Graduation from Boise CodeWorks will be in late April 2019.
